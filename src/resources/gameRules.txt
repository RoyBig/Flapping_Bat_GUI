In this game, your objective is
to navigate without crashing into
the walls or the ground.

Your character has
the ability to leap into the air
by simply tapping the space bar.
Otherwise, the bat will
fall and hit the ground.

To pause a game you have to
click ESC button.

After each game you can enter
your nickname to save your score
in the leaderboards, where
top 10 scores are listed.
